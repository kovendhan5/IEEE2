Workshop on data visualization using PowerBI

Join us for an immersive Workshop on Data Visualization Using Power BI, where you'll gain hands-on experience and earn two valuable certifications: a participation certificate and a project completion certificate. The workshop kicks off with a morning session focused on teaching you how to effectively use Power BI, guided by expert instructors. In the afternoon, you'll put your skills to the test by working on a project using a real dataset. The best project will be rewarded, offering you a chance to showcase your data visualization expertise. This is an excellent opportunity to enhance your skills, earn recognized certifications, and make your mark with a standout project.

Hackathon

Join us for the Tech x JIT Hackathon, a thrilling two-day event where creativity meets innovation.
On Day 1, participants will form teams and choose from a variety of compelling problem statements. Once teams are set, the brainstorming begins as you work together to devise innovative solutions.
Day 2 is all about bringing your ideas to life. Teams will develop a prototype of their solution and present it to a panel of expert judges. The event will culminate in an exciting closing ceremony, where the best projects will be recognized, and winners awarded with exciting cash prizes. Whether you're a seasoned coder or a creative thinker, this hackathon is the perfect opportunity to showcase your skills, collaborate with peers, and make a mark in the tech community. Don’t miss out on this chance to innovate, compete, and win big!
